Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Crisp (referred to as "we," "our," or "the app"). Crisp is your intelligent personal lifestyle management assistant, designed to help you organize your life efficiently through AI-powered interactions. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use Crisp.

2. Information We Collect

  • Item Data: Information about items you manage through the app, including names, descriptions, dates, and status.
  • Voice Data: Audio recordings of your voice inputs, which are processed by AI to understand your requests. Voice data is used for transcription and AI interaction purposes.
  • Image Data: Photos you capture using the camera feature to scan or identify items. Images are processed by AI services for recognition and then handled according to our data retention policies.
  • Account Information: Basic information provided through Firebase Authentication (such as email address) used to sync your data across devices.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app to improve our services.

4. Third-Party Services

To provide our services, we use the following trusted infrastructure providers:

  • Google Firebase: Used for secure data storage, user authentication, and data synchronization across your devices.
  • AI/LLM Service Providers: Used for intelligent analysis of your inputs, natural language processing, image recognition, and generating helpful suggestions.

These services are bound by their own privacy policies and security standards. We carefully select partners who maintain high standards of data protection.

5. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the core functionality of Crisp
  • To process your voice and image inputs through AI services
  • To sync your data across your devices
  • To send you notifications and reminders
  • To improve and optimize our services
  • To provide customer support

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your information is encrypted in transit and at rest. We regularly review and update our security practices to ensure your data remains protected.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so in the app's Settings page by selecting "Delete Account," or by contacting us via email. Upon account deletion, all your personal data will be permanently removed from our systems.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

9. Children's Privacy

Crisp is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
